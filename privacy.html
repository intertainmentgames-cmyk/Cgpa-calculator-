Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Our UET CGPA Calculator tool is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identity information. Any data you enter, such as your name, department, grades, or credit hours, is processed strictly locally on your device to calculate your GPA/CGPA.

2. How We Use Your Data

The calculations are performed entirely in your web browser. No data is transmitted to any external servers. Your name or inputs are only used to personalize your current calculation session and generate your instant results.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We may use basic browser local storage or cookies solely to save your department selection or preferences so that you do not have to re-enter them on your next visit. This data stays securely on your own device.

4. Security

Since your information never leaves your device, it remains completely secure under your control. We are committed to ensuring your academic calculation experience is safe and private.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our tool. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with a revised updated date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, feel free to contact us through our website support link.
