Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How is GPA calculated at UET?
Your GPA is calculated by multiplying the grade points of each subject by its credit hours, adding them up, and then dividing the total by the sum of all credit hours for that semester.
Q2: What is the difference between GPA and CGPA?
GPA is for a single semester, whereas CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the combined average of all the semesters you have completed so far.
Q3: Do Mechanical Engineering students need to type subjects manually?
No! For Mechanical Engineering, the subjects and their credit hours are already pre-loaded according to the official UET curriculum. You just need to select your grades.
Q4: Can students from other departments use this calculator?
Yes, absolutely! Just choose the "Custom / Other Department" option to manually enter your subjects, credit hours, and grades to get instant results.
